Party: Purple



  • Gun Control

    • B is in favor of a licensing system for gun ownership. B stated, “Guns are a major responsibility and thus we need to have common-sense gun safety measures, especially considering that there are already approaching 400 million firearms in the United States. Responsible gun owners should continue to enjoy the right to bear arms, subject to licensing and education requirements that will enhance public safety. But the most dangerous weapons that make mass shootings as deadly as they have become should be banned. ”



  • Climate

    • B believes that climate change is an existential threat, and that we must recognize we’re already living through the negative effects. B plans to build a sustainable economy by transitioning away from fossil fuels to renewable energy, upgrading our infrastructure, and improving the way we farm and use land. B also plans to reverse the damage we’ve done by researching on how to remove carbon from our atmosphere, cooling the planet, and rejuvenating ecosystems.



  • Abortion

    • B is pro-choice, meaning B supports the right to abortion. B believes providing birth control, sex education, and more social services will help reduce the number of abortions. On his website, B stated, “In a perfect world, only those who are ready for and desire a child would become pregnant. But that’s not the world we live in.” (yang2020.com)



  • LGBT+

    • B is a big supporter of the LGBTQ+ community. B believes “sexual orientation and gender identity should be protected classes under the law, receiving all the federal protections afforded under the Constitution and law.” (yang2020.com)
